About

Seth Markwood is a Revenue Growth Director at Thermaco, Inc., where he specializes in helping restaurants and commercial kitchens overcome their grease management challenges. Based in High Point, North Carolina, Seth combines technical expertise with a passion for customer success.

Professional Journey

With a unique background that spans education, ministry, and business development, Seth brings a multifaceted approach to the commercial kitchen industry. His experience includes roles at Thermaco, Inc. (Big Dipper / Trapzilla division), PF Plumbing, and Southwest Guilford High School, where he works as a one-on-one assistant.

Education & Personal Life

Seth holds a Master of Arts in Religion/Religious Studies from Liberty University (2009-2010). An ordained pastor who is actively planting a church, he brings values of service and community to his professional work. He lives in Greensboro, North Carolina, with his wife Allison and son Ender.

Mission

At the intersection of technology and hospitality, Seth's mission is simple yet profound: help restaurants beat grease so they can get back to wowing their customers.
