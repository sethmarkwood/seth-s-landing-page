Thermaco & Grease Solutions

Thermaco, Inc. stands as the leading separation technology company in the highly specialized field of oil and grease extraction from commercial kitchen wastewater. Founded in Asheboro, North Carolina, in 1983, the company has revolutionized how restaurants and commercial kitchens manage their grease and food waste.

Industry-Leading Products

Big Dipper Automatic Grease Removal Device

The Big Dipper represents the best-selling automatic grease trap in the United States. This innovative system provides continuous, automatic removal of grease from kitchen wastewater, ensuring consistent compliance with local regulations while minimizing maintenance requirements.

Trapzilla Super-Capacity Grease Interceptor

Designed for high-volume operations, Trapzilla offers superior grease retention capacity in a compact footprint. Its advanced design allows for easier installation and maintenance compared to traditional concrete interceptors.

The Grease Management Challenge

Commercial kitchens face strict water and sewer regulations that prohibit fats, oils, grease (FOG), and solid kitchen waste from entering the wastewater system. Non-compliance can result in:

  • Costly fines and penalties
  • Sewer blockages and overflows
  • Environmental damage
  • Business interruptions
  • Reputation damage

Comprehensive Solutions

Beyond grease interceptors and traps, Thermaco manufactures a complete line of pretreatment accessories including:

  • Sampling ports for regulatory compliance
  • Flow control devices
  • Alarm systems for overflow prevention
  • Custom solutions for unique applications
